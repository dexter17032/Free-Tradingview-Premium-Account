INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Press the Start Button to begin the game.
  2. The device will play a sequence by lighting up and playing a tone for each colored button. Watch and listen carefully.
  3. After the device has played the sequence, it's your turn to repeat it. Press the buttons in the same order as the device.
  4. If you successfully repeat the sequence, the device will add a new color to the pattern. Your goal is to remember and repeat the entire sequence correctly.
  5. If you make a mistake and press the wrong button or get the order wrong, the game will signal the mistake, and you'll need to start over.
  6. The game continues with longer and more complex sequences as you successfully repeat them.
